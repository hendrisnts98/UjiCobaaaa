Hi, welcome to vocabulary

Vocabulary is all the words you understand,
hear, read, write and speak in a language.

Types of Vocabulary

Active Vocabulary:
Vocabulary that you frequently use when speaking or writing.
Examples: eat, go, study, work

Passive Vocabulary:
Vocabulary that you understand when reading or hearing,
but rarely use yourself.
Examples: sophisticated, tremendous, nevertheless

The Importance of Learning Vocabulary

1. Helps you understand English reading and conversation.

2. Improves your ability to express yourself in English.

3. Increases your confidence in using English.

Examples of Basic Vocabulary in English

Example Meaning
Book Buku
Happy Bahagia
Eat Makan

Tips to Increase Vocabulary

1. Read often (articles, short stories, novels).

2. Listen to English songs, podcasts, or audiobooks.

3. Practice speaking with friends or language partners.
