Definition

Simple Past Tense is used to express events or activities
that have occurred in the past and have been completed.

General Characteristics:

Using Verb 2 (past form)

Do not use “is/am/are” for action verbs

Use "did" for negative and interrogative sentences.

Formula

Positive Sentences (+)

Subject Verb Object
Subject Verb 2 (past form) Object

Example: I went to the market yesterday.

Formula

Negative Sentences (–)

Subject did not (didn't) Verb Object
Subject am/did not (didn't) Verb 1 Object

Example: I did not (didn't) go to the market yesterday.

Formula

Interrogative Sentence (?)

Did Subject Verb Object
Did Subject Verb 1 Object?

Example: Did you go to the market yesterday?
